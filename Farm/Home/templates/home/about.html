About KrishiSetu

Who We Are

KrishiSetu is a transformative digital marketplace that connects farmers, retailers, and customers in a transparent and fair ecosystem. We empower farmers to secure better prices by eliminating intermediaries and enable retailers and consumers to access fresh, quality produce directly from the source.

Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the agricultural supply chain by creating a trusted, data-driven platform that facilitates direct transactions, fair pricing, and real-time market intelligence. We aim to foster sustainable growth in agriculture, ensuring that every stakeholder benefits from a streamlined, secure, and efficient marketplace.

About the Application

KrishiSetu leverages advanced technology—including AI-driven market analysis, dynamic pricing models, and integrated logistics—to provide a comprehensive solution for the modern agricultural market. Our platform not only offers real-time price discovery and secure transactions but also categorizes produce based on quality certifications, enabling premium pricing for organically grown goods.
